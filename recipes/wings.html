Creamy Spinach Artichoke Pizza Recipe

This is a delicious pizza that replaces the cheese!

  • 4 lbs chicken wings
  • 5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoons salt
  • 4 Tbsp unsalted butter, mixed
  • 1/2 cup Frank's Original Hot Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 clove small garlic, minced
  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Black pepper
  • Celery sticks
  1. If you plan ahead, lay the wings on a rack on a rimmed baking tray and let them dry uncovered in the fridge overnight. Alternatively, pat them dry with paper towels.
  2. Preheat the oven to 250째F/120째C (all oven types). Put one oven shelf in the lower quarter of the oven and one in the top quarter.
  3. Line the tray with foil. Spray the rack on the baking tray with oil.
  4. Place the wings in a large bowl. Sprinkle over baking powder and salt. Use hands to toss well (or shake in ziplock bag) to coat wings evenly./li>
  5. Place the wings skin side up on the rack (ok if snug, they shrink). Bake on the lower shelf in the oven for 30 minutes.
  6. Then move the tray up to the higher shelf and turn the oven up to 425째F / 220째C. Bake for a further 40 to 50 minutes, rotating the tray halfway through. The wings do not need to be turned over.
  7. Toss with Hot Sauce and serve immediately with Blue Cheese Dip and celery sticks. They can be served plain, with a dipping sauce or tossed in sauce. Wings also reheat crispy!
  8. The wings are ready when they are dark golden brown and the skin is very crispy.
